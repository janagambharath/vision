FAQ

Common questions before a visit or frame booking.

The website keeps medical care central while making frame selection and WhatsApp follow-up faster.

Can I book an eye test before buying frames?

Yes. The appointment form and WhatsApp links are built around consultation first, then lens and frame selection.

How many frames can I choose for home trial?

You can select up to five frames. If you select multiple frames, the home-trial option is highlighted in the summary.

Does virtual try-on replace a prescription check?

No. Try-on helps with frame appearance and fit preference. Prescription and lens guidance should still come from the clinic.

Can I send my try-on photo to WhatsApp?

Yes. If direct file sharing is supported by the device, the image can be shared. Otherwise, WhatsApp opens with the lead details and the image can be downloaded.
