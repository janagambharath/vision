Retina Services

Specialized Retina Care & Treatment

Retinal conditions need timely diagnosis and expert care. Our clinic offers advanced retinal evaluation, intravitreal treatments, and surgical interventions managed by experienced specialists.

👁️
Diabetic Retinopathy
Comprehensive evaluation and management of retinal changes caused by diabetes — from early screening to advanced laser and injection treatment.
💉
Intravitreal Injections
Anti-VEGF and steroid injections for macular degeneration, diabetic macular edema, and retinal vascular conditions — administered with precision.
🏥
Vitrectomy Surgery
Advanced vitreoretinal surgery for conditions like vitreous hemorrhage, epiretinal membrane, and macular hole — restoring retinal function.
🎯
Retinal Detachment Treatment
Urgent surgical care for retinal detachment — a time-sensitive condition requiring immediate expert attention to preserve your vision.

⚠️ When to See a Retina Specialist: Sudden flashes, floaters, shadow in vision, or blurred sight after trauma — these are warning signs. Visit us immediately for evaluation.

Glaucoma Care

Expert Glaucoma Evaluation & Treatment

Glaucoma — often called the "silent thief of sight" — requires early detection, precise monitoring, and timely intervention. Our clinic offers a complete glaucoma management pathway.

Glaucoma Management Services

1
OCT (Optical Coherence Tomography)
High-resolution imaging of optic nerve and retinal fiber layers
2
Visual Fields Testing
Automated perimetry to map peripheral vision and detect field loss
3
Gonioscopy
Evaluation of the drainage angle of the eye to classify glaucoma type
4
Trabeculectomy
Surgical procedure to create a new drainage pathway and reduce eye pressure
5
Valve Surgeries
Ahmed / Baerveldt implants for complex or refractory glaucoma management

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about our services, procedures, and what to expect.

Cataract surgery is recommended when the lens cloudiness significantly affects your daily vision — difficulty reading, driving, or recognizing faces. A consultation and scan will confirm whether surgery is the right next step for you.
A monofocal lens provides clear vision at one distance (usually far), meaning you may still need reading glasses. A multifocal or trifocal lens provides vision at multiple distances, significantly reducing or eliminating dependence on glasses. The right choice depends on your lifestyle, retina health, and doctor evaluation.
A LASIK evaluation includes corneal topography (Orbscan/Pentacam), corneal thickness measurement, pupil assessment, and a detailed prescription check to determine if you are a suitable candidate for laser vision correction. Yes — we offer this evaluation completely FREE at Vision Vistara.
Our glaucoma evaluation includes OCT (Optical Coherence Tomography) for optic nerve imaging, automated visual fields testing, gonioscopy for drainage angle assessment, and intraocular pressure measurement. These together give us a complete picture of your glaucoma status.
Seek immediate evaluation if you experience sudden flashes of light, a significant increase in floaters, a shadow or curtain across your vision, or sudden loss of central or peripheral vision. These could indicate retinal detachment or other urgent conditions that require prompt treatment.
