FAQ

Common questions before a visit or frame booking.

The website keeps medical care central while making frame selection and WhatsApp follow-up faster.

Can I book an eye test before buying frames?

Yes. The appointment form and WhatsApp links are built around consultation first, then lens and frame selection.

How many frames can I choose for home trial?

You can select up to five frames. A Rs. 199 service fee applies, with a refundable Rs. 500 deposit if you choose three or more frames.

Does virtual try-on replace a prescription check?

No. Try-on helps with frame appearance and fit preference. Prescription and lens guidance should still come from the clinic.

Can I send my try-on photo to WhatsApp?

Yes. After generating your try-on, the lead form opens WhatsApp with your frame details. You can also download the image separately and attach it.

What lens options are available?

The clinic offers basic clear lenses, anti-reflective coating, blue-light filter lenses, and photochromic (Transitions) lenses. Pricing is shown in the frame selection panel. Final lens recommendation is confirmed at the clinic based on your prescription.

Is the clinic address and parking available?

The clinic is at Plot 4, Opposite ECIL X Roads, Uppal Main Road, Hyderabad – 500039. Call 7842938316 before visiting to confirm parking and appointment availability.
